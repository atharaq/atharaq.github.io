Welcome!

Course Format

  • Online Combined
  • Asynchronous: content posted Mondays.
    • Combination of videos, readings, online homework
    • “Lectures”
  • Synchronous meetings: Thursdays 12:30 - 2:10
    • “Problem sessions”
    • “Reviews”

Textbook / Software

  • OpenStax Calculus Vol 2 free
  • Scanner app: to submit written homework assignments on Moodle
  • Zoom, for weekly synchronous meetings / office hours
  • MyOpenMath: online homework (free)
  • Discord: group chat / help.
  • More info on all of the above on Moodle.

Expectations

  • 12 hours of work / week
  • 2 hours synchronous (Thursdays), 10 hours “everything else”
  • Studying, reviewing videos, but mostly: working through problems
  • Stuck? Discord / email / office hours
  • Sync meetings: prepare ahead of time.
    • Problems / topics / concepts you need help with
    • Problems / topics / concepts you can help your classmates with

Grading

  • 30% Written homeworks, quizzes, class participation (includes completion of asynchronous classwork)
  • 20% Online homework
  • 20% Problem Presentations (4-6)
  • 15% Mid-semester exams (3)
  • 15% Final Exam

All exams are open book and open notes. You will be given several days to complete them.
